Instant Pot Umami Chili

Description

We're making some instant pot umami chili yummy yummy in my tummy. I love chili because it has a lot of protein and it stores well in the fridge. Some would even say it gets better!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef
  2. Mix chicken stock mixture
  3. Saute onion, garlic, and spices
  4. Deglace
  5. Pressure cook chili
  6. Reduce and season
